Support Animals in Martinique: Your Donation Makes a Difference!

In Martinique, the situation of domestic animals, especially dogs and cats, remains concerning. Despite legal advances in combating mistreatment, many challenges persist.

Key Issues

Mistreatment and Neglect: Dogs, often used for guarding, are sometimes tied up without proper care. Cats, mainly used for rat hunting, are also neglected.

Stray Animal Problem: Stray dogs and cats pose public health and safety issues.

SOS Animaux de Compagnie Association: Established in 2008, this volunteer organization is dedicated to rescuing mistreated or abandoned animals. It provides essential veterinary care and seeks adoptive homes, primarily in mainland France.

Impact of Your Help

Essential Financial Support: Every donation contributes to funding veterinary care, rescue, and housing for animals.

Tax Advantage: For French tax residents, your donation is deductible from taxes up to 66%, enhancing the impact of your generosity.
